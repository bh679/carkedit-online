# Game Rules — Carked It! Online

## Introduction

Carked It! is a party card game where players plan their life, death, and afterlife
using three decks of cards. Players propose what they think is best for each other
and earn points when their suggestion is chosen. A bonus Eulogy round awards final
points. The player with the most points is crowned the Legendary Death Doula.

The game contains three decks:
- **Die deck** (Yellow) — 48 cards describing how you die
- **Live deck** (Green) — 68 cards for things to do before you die
- **Bye deck** (Blue) — 68 cards for what happens after you die

The game has four phases:
1. **Phase 1 — Die!** — Draw a death card (no points)
2. **Phase 2 — Live!** — Choose how to live before you cark it (points awarded)
3. **Phase 3 — Bye!** — Choose what happens after death (points awarded)
4. **Phase 4 — Eulogy Wildcard** — Bonus round for extra points

## Setup

1. **Nominate a Funeral Director** — One player is designated as the Funeral Director.
   They keep the game on track, maintain the score tally, and ensure all players
   understand the rules. In the online version, this role is handled by the host.

2. **Set Up the Decks** — Separate the Live, Die, and Bye cards into three individual
   decks. Each deck is shuffled and placed face-down in the centre of play.

3. **Choose Who Goes First** — The player whose birthday is closest to the Day of the
   Dead (November 1st) is Player 1 and goes first. Play proceeds clockwise.

## Phase 1 — Die!

Starting with Player 1, each player draws a Die card from the deck and places it
face up in front of them. Players take turns describing their death out loud.

**Special Die cards:**
- **Split "Would You Rather" cards** — If you draw a split card, tell all players
  which death on the card you prefer and why.
- **Question Mark cards** — If you draw a question mark card, tell everyone your
  death option using the prompt on the card.

No points are awarded in this phase.

## Phase 2 — Live!

Decide how you want to live your life before you cark it!

1. The Funeral Director deals 5 Live cards face-down to each player. Players may
   look at their hand but must keep it hidden from other players.

2. Player 1 is the first to choose. All other players select 1 Live card from their
   hand and place it face down on the table.

3. Once everyone has placed a card, all players reveal their card and take turns
   convincing Player 1 that their choice is the best option. Argue, persuade, be
   sensible, silly, or stupendous!

4. Player 1 chooses their favourite Live card and places it to the left of their Die
   card. The player who put forward the winning card is awarded 1 point. The Funeral
   Director keeps a score tally.

5. All Live cards that were not selected return to the bottom of the Live deck. Each
   player draws back up to 5 Live cards in their hand.

6. Play continues with the next player. Phase 2 is repeated until all players have
   chosen 2 Live cards.

## Phase 3 — Bye!

Decide what you want to happen after you die!

1. All players discard all remaining Live cards from their hands to the bottom of the
   Live card deck.

2. The Funeral Director deals 5 Bye cards face-down to each player. Players may look
   at their hand but must keep it hidden from other players.

3. Similar to Phase 2, Player 1 is the first to decide on their after-death
   preferences. All other players place 1 Bye card from their hand face down, then
   take turns revealing their choice and convincing Player 1 that their option is
   the best for them.

4. Player 1 chooses their favourite Bye card and places it to the right of their Die
   card. The player who put forward the winning card is awarded 1 point.

5. Phase 3 is repeated until all players have chosen 2 Bye cards to the right of
   their Die card.

## Phase 4 — Eulogy Wildcard

Once all players have 2 Bye cards next to their Die card, the Eulogy Wildcard round
begins.

1. If a player has a Wildcard Eulogy card in their remaining Bye hand, they may play
   it face up in front of them for a chance to earn and award bonus points.

2. The player who played the Wildcard picks two other players to give their eulogy.

3. Those two players go head to head, each spending 30 seconds orating, celebrating,
   and honouring the memory of the player who played the card. Feel free to throw in
   what you really think of them!

4. The player who played the Wildcard judges the eulogies:
   - Best eulogy is awarded **2 points**
   - Runner-up is awarded **1 point**
   - The player who played the Eulogy card also receives **1 point**

## Winning

After all Eulogy Wildcards have been played, the Funeral Director reveals which
player earned the most points. That player is crowned the **Legendary Death Doula!**

## Short Version

For a shorter game, play until each player has 1 Live card and 1 Bye card next to
their Die card (instead of 2 each).

## Card Layout Reference

At the end of the game, each player's tableau looks like this:

```
LIVE  LIVE  DIE  BYE  BYE
```

- Live cards are placed to the **left** of the Die card
- Bye cards are placed to the **right** of the Die card
- In the short version, each player has 1 Live, 1 Die, and 1 Bye card
